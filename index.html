Getting Started

How to Play

1
Set up the ring
Marbles are placed inside the circular board at the start of each match.
2
Drag to aim
Drag backwards from your shooter marble like a slingshot to set your direction and power.
3
Release to shoot
Let go to fire your shooter. Knock opponent marbles out of the ring to capture them.
4
Most captures wins
The player who knocks out the most marbles when the ring is empty wins the match.
